  • Our Background

    • Irenic Bibliotheca is an organisation that was created to supply information to the users. The term "irenic" suggests something peaceful or promoting peace, and "bibliotheca" is Latin for library.

    Our objective in introducing LinkedIn to the user are to support their career development, enhance networking opportunities and improve digital literacy.

    This helps users build a professional online presence, access mentorship and job opportunities, gain market insights, develop new skills through LinkedIn learning and leverage premium resources available at libraries. This initiative equips students with the necessary tools for success in the modern job market
